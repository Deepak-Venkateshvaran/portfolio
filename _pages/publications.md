Our work focuses on understanding the physics of disordered semiconductors: small molecules, polymers, hybrids, and the like. We implement precision measurement techniques and fabricate prototype devices within research themes of <code style="color : red">thermoelectrics</code>, <code style="color : green">charge transport</code>, <code style="color : blue">spintronics</code>, and <code style="color : darkorange">nanomechanics</code>.  
My work has translational impact on <code style="color : purple">science policy</code> in UK Government.  

Here is a selection of key publications where I played a major role.
